Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started on Skill Exchange?
Getting started is easy! Simply register for an account, complete your profile with your skills and experience, and you're ready to browse jobs or post your own. We recommend adding a profile picture and detailed information to build trust with clients and workers.
What are coins and how do they work?
Coins are the virtual currency used on Skill Exchange. Clients use coins to pay for completed work, and workers earn coins for their services. When a job is posted, the coins are locked in escrow. Once the work is approved, the coins are released to the worker's wallet. You can withdraw coins or use them to hire other workers.
How do I apply for a job?
Browse available jobs in your area of expertise, click on any job to view details, and click the "Apply" button. Write a compelling proposal explaining why you're the best fit for the job. Include relevant experience, estimated completion time, and any questions you have about the project.
How do I post a job?
Click "Post Job" in the navigation menu, fill in the job details including title, description, category, deadline, and coin payment. Make sure to provide clear requirements and deliverables. The coins will be locked from your wallet until the job is completed and approved.
What happens after I submit my work?
After submitting your work, the client will review it. If approved, the locked coins are automatically released to your wallet. If revisions are needed, the client will provide feedback. You can communicate through the messaging system to clarify any requirements.
How do I build my reputation?
Build your reputation by completing jobs on time, delivering quality work, and maintaining good communication with clients. After each completed job, clients can rate your work. Higher ratings and more completed jobs increase your visibility and trustworthiness on the platform.
Is my payment secure?
Yes! When a client posts a job, the payment is locked in escrow. This ensures workers that payment is available. The coins are only released when the client approves the work, protecting both parties in the transaction.
Can I cancel a job?
Clients can cancel jobs before a worker is assigned. Once work has started, cancellation requires mutual agreement. If there's a dispute, you can contact our support team for assistance. Always communicate clearly with the other party before canceling.
